Instructions:

Please set the Aspect Ratio to "Full HD" in Unity.
Open Game/Assets/Scenes/SampleScene.unity to play

Use keyboard to play this game:
--A/D: player move left / move right
--W/S: player speed up / slow down
--Space: Jump

Objects on Screen:
--Coin: Add 1 point,
--Mushroom: Add 5 points, and player's appearance will change for a few seconds,
--Wood: Hitting a wood will slow down the player,

Win Condition:
--Reach the finish line.
Lose Condition:
--Fail to reach the finish line.
